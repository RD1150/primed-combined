Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 3, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Mindrocket Systems LLC, a California limited liability company doing business as Primed ("we," "us," or "our"), collects, uses, shares, and protects information about you when you use our website at primed.today, our application (the "Service"), and related services.

We've written this in plain English. Where the law requires specific terms, we use them — but we've tried to explain what they mean.

The short version: We collect your email and account information so the Service works. We use Stripe for payments (we never see or store your card data). We use AI providers (Anthropic, ElevenLabs) to generate scenarios and voice practice. We don't sell your data. You can delete your account at any time.

2. Information we collect

Information you provide directly

  • Account information: when you create an account, we collect your email address, name (if provided), and a password (stored only as a salted bcrypt hash — we never see your plaintext password).
  • Waitlist signups: if you join our pre-launch waitlist on the Primed homepage, we collect your email address through ConvertKit.
  • Subscription and billing information: when you subscribe, payment information (card number, billing address) is collected and processed directly by Stripe. We receive only a customer ID, subscription status, and the last four digits of your card; we do not see or store your full card details.
  • User-generated content: any custom scenarios you create, prompts you write, or notes you save inside the Service.
  • Voice recordings (optional): if you use the AI practice mode with voice, your audio is sent to ElevenLabs to generate AI voice responses. We do not retain your raw audio after the practice session ends.
  • Communications with us: if you email us at privacy@primed.today or other addresses, we retain those messages so we can respond.

Information we collect automatically

  • Authentication cookie / token: when you log in, we store a JSON Web Token (JWT) in your browser's local storage so you stay logged in between sessions. This token expires and is removed when you log out.
  • Invite cookie (pre-launch only): during our pre-launch period, accessing the Service may set a long-lived browser cookie named primed_invite so your invite link only needs to be used once. This cookie contains no personal data.
  • Server logs: our hosting provider (Render) automatically logs request metadata including IP address, user agent, request path, and timestamp for security and operational purposes. These logs are retained for a limited period and are not used for advertising or profiling.

Information we do NOT currently collect

We currently do not use third-party analytics, advertising trackers, or session-replay tools. We do not use cookies for marketing or behavioral profiling. If we add analytics in the future, we'll update this Policy and notify users.

3. How we use your information

  • To provide and operate the Service: authenticate you, deliver scenarios, save your progress, generate AI content you request.
  • To process payments and manage subscriptions: through Stripe.
  • To communicate with you: send transactional emails (password resets, billing receipts, account notices), and — if you opt in — pre-launch updates and product announcements via ConvertKit. You can unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time.
  • To improve the Service: understand which scenarios are used most, identify bugs, prioritize features. We do this in aggregate and without selling individual data.
  • To comply with law and protect rights: respond to lawful requests, enforce our Terms of Service, and prevent fraud or abuse.

5. Cookies and similar technologies

We use the following cookies and storage mechanisms:

  • Auth token (localStorage): required for login. Removed when you log out.

We do not use third-party cookies, advertising cookies, or analytics cookies at this time. If we add any in the future, we will update this Policy and provide a cookie notice.

6. Data retention

  • Account data: retained as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we delete your account data within 30 days, except where retention is required for legal, billing, or fraud-prevention purposes.
  • Billing records: retained for at least 7 years for tax and accounting compliance.
  • Server logs: retained for up to 90 days.
  • Email correspondence: retained as long as reasonably necessary to address your inquiries.
  • AI prompt history: retained while your account is active so you can revisit prior generations; deleted with account deletion.

7. Data security

We use commercially reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including:

  • HTTPS / TLS encryption for all data in transit;
  • Encrypted storage at rest for our database;
  • Bcrypt-hashed passwords (we never see plaintext);
  • Limited internal access on a need-to-know basis;
  • Subprocessor agreements with security obligations.

No system is 100% secure. If a security incident affects your data, we will notify you in accordance with applicable law.

8. Your privacy rights

If you are a California resident, you have the following rights:

  • Right to know: request the categories and specific pieces of personal information we have collected about you, the sources, the purposes for collection, and the categories of third parties with whom we share it.
  • Right to delete: request that we delete your personal information, subject to certain exceptions (e.g., legal compliance, fraud prevention).
  • Right to correct: request that we correct inaccurate personal information.
  • Right to opt out of sale or sharing: we do not sell your personal information and do not share it for cross-context behavioral advertising. There is nothing to opt out of, but if our practices change, we will provide an opt-out mechanism.
  • Right to limit use of sensitive personal information: we do not collect or use sensitive personal information beyond what is necessary to provide the Service.
  • Right to non-discrimination: we will not deny service, charge different prices, or provide a different level of service because you exercised any of these rights.

To exercise any of these rights, email privacy@primed.today from the email address associated with your account, or write to us at the postal address above. We will verify your identity and respond within 45 days as required by law.
